MY STARTING TOWN - WHERE THE ADVENTURE BEGINS

My artistic passions, especially for performance arts, have become an inherent part of my life ever since I learned to draw and dance in preschool. Growing up, I went from dance class to art lessons to film competitions, never once doubting my pursuit of such disciplines. Even though they are no longer my main goals, they are still actively present in my life, and have a major role in shaping who I am today. As an amateur cheerleader, dancer, model and film critique, my story is now more colorful than ever before.

Cheerleading

MAJOR POWER-UP: CHEER AMS CAPTAIN

As the captain of Hanoi-Amsterdam High School’s one and only cheerleading squad, “Cheer Ams”, I have had a great many experiences with leadership and problem-solving. Even in the face of numerous hardships, we were able to stay strong, work together, and achieve the impossible, upholding our name as one of the most popular clubs in the school as well as the most well-known high school cheerleading team in Hanoi ever since our debut in 2008.

“AMS GOT TALENT” SHOW

“Ams Got Talent”, or AGT, is  is Hanoi-Amsterdam High School’s annual talent competition, attracting participation from over 60 teams each year. During my term in Cheer Ams, we were able to achieve the Champion Special Prize and the Second Prize in this competition.

G’LAMS MUSICAL

As one of the most well-known high school musicals in Hanoi, G’LAMS Musical has been ongoing for over 11 years, during which I have had the honor of participating in two productions as a dancer.

In G’LAMS 2024, with the plot surrounding the topic of the nine-tailed fox legend from Japan, I had the chance to transform into a Japanese student, a rabbit, and a mythical fox. With the harmonious cooperation of the actors, dancers, backstage crew, and music, the story fully portrayed the fascinating cultures and tales of Japan, along with the complex conscience encompassed within both humans and animals alike.

In G’LAMS 2025, we embraced the beauty of our own culture and took inspiration from the Vietnamese Tày minority’s traditions for our plot. With the complexity of the story incorporated with the dance team’s elaborate movements and the actor’s emotional range, we were able to paint the portrait of the human’s nuance and sophistication within the 2-hour musical, setting the stage for more successes to come.

Film analysis

NEW SKILL UNLOCKED: FILM ANALYSIS

Aside from my love for performing arts, I also find deep enjoyment in film studies, particularly analyzing the cinematography and moral aspects of movies. Last year, I participated in the Immerse Essay Competition, in which I submitted an essay surrounding the topic of “How do contemporary filmmakers use cinematic techniques to address social and political issues?”. My essay received a Merit Award, from which point on, I unlocked a newfound zeal for cinematic arts. ARTIFACT COLLECTED: VISUAL SHOOTS & POEMS

Combining my love for modeling, photography, and writing poetic expressions, I decided to create this collection of photoshoots and their respective accompanying poems as a tribute to each of my passions: humanities, cheerleading, mythology, culture, and nature’s beauty—each of which an unforgettable venture.

LIBERATION

“These walls were built with frightened hands,
Afraid of where wild danger stands
But freedom called out, loud and clear
For one to break away their fears.

By tremble and tremor,
By quiver and terror,
The mask unveiled—
The first in forever.”

Opened were eyes that used to close,
Unveiling the sun ’neath fear’s repose.
For life had bloomed before one’s gaze
Once nearly lost within the haze.

For beauty never hides nor flees,
It waits for souls that dare to see.”

TALE OF THE NINE-TAILED

“Beneath the moon’s unblinking eye,
Masked by the mist under the sky,
There lay a whisper—soft, divine,
A spirit born of myth and time.

Her fur a flame, her eyes a sea
That drowns the soul in reverie.
Her nine long tails, each one a tale—
Nine secrets yet to be unveiled.

In moonlit haze she weaves her song,
Where magic, dreams, and stars belong.
She is the dusk, the dawn, the in-between,
The unseen grace, vast and serene.”
